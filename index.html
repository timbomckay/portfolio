About Me

I'm a designer and developer working in the industry for nearly a decade. I focus on the user for a rewarding experience, making the interactions simple and as little room for error as possible. I enjoy implementing new technologies and standards into projects in order to modernize and optimize the performance of site for faster interactions.

While I have plenty of experience designing websites, I love collaborating with great designers that can focus on their art while I offer some trusted feedback as a developer with a strong sensibility for design. Designers like working with me because I put a level of care and attention into bringing their designs to life. I'm proactive in implementing the look/feel that they've created across the site, which means less back-and-forth, less meetings, and a greater ROI for the business. I’m independently driven to get my work done and prefer working with a team for collaboration, whether that’s advice with a feature I’m working on or tackling different obstacles on the same project.
