November 2016

Created a WordPress site to display a list of articles, defining a pattern for categories to be listed in the array with a conditional for filler tiles to complete the grid. Featuring an AJAX loader to get more posts with the same pattern array which updates the url so the back & refresh buttons operate appropriately. Focused primarily on presenting the article as most visitors will be coming from the newsletter or social media straight to the article, designing a content-centered layout so the reader is free from distractions and a pleasant reading experience.

KET

2015 - 2016

Most of my contributions for KET was for their legislature section. I took their data source for showtimes and incorporated it into the site with php & javascript functionality for best viewing options, using the api to list out previous, current and future episodes.

I've also made various style updates to the site as a whole. Collaborated on the top live-banner to notify the visitor of shows currently airing and integrating cookies to hide a program if the visitor wasn't interested. Another task was debugging their Google Ads and creating an easier system within WordPress to manage their sidebars relative to specific categories and pages.
